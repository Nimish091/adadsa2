Key observations
Very high rejection rate

83 of 89 applicants rejected (~93%), indicating the limiting factor is quality and fit, not top-of-funnel volume. The job post has been continuously active.

Most rejections at earliest stage

65 (~78%) rejected at application stage — screening criteria are filtering heavily, or inbound is broadly misaligned with the actual role profile.

Rare conversion to late stages

Only 1 candidate reached a final-stage rejection. Rejections are spread across Screening (11), Intro (3), Technical Assessment (3), and Final (1) — confirming conversion is very low throughout the funnel.

⚠️ Recruitment challenges
🔴
Talent scarcity — limited pool of EMs in Croatia; a substantial share has already been engaged, narrowing the addressable market quickly.
🟡
Role requirement — need technically strong candidates who are early in their management careers. This narrows the already limited pool further.
🔵
Industry mismatch — remaining candidates often come from less-aligned industries, reducing pipeline quality at the top of funnel.
